Description

A’Lo!

Welcome on to your very own local shopping app A-Lo that connects you with your neighborhood stores. A-Lo gives you the most satisfying experience of shopping allowing you to EXPLORE the products that you are looking for NEAR YOU with an option to check the product’s AVAILABILITY from anywhere at your convenience.

The above features allow our customers to locate and directly connects them with the stores within no time. Therefore, saves you the time, money and of course energy.

A-Lo is Simple to use and easy to shop with option for uncomplicated collection and delivery services.

EXPLORE: Look and search for your favorite products in the neighborhood with detailed info at your convenience.Look > Explore > Details

NEAR ME: Once you chose the product, you can locate the store simply from your phone, pick it up when you can.

Details > Near Me > Stores

AVAILABILITY: Irrespective of the price and size of the product, we make sure to ensure excellent service to our customers. Confirm the availability of the products and save a round trip to the store for nothing. Stores > Availability > Shop

DELIVERY: After an exciting virtual shopping tour, walk into the store and check the products for the quality. You can either collect or get them delivered to your door step. Shop > Delivery options > Ready to Use

About Us

My name is Rajnikanth, I had been working as a product designer for the last four years. All of this began one day with multiple thoughts wandering in my head and one of those stayed with me, took a shape which is now called as A’lo App.

My search continued to design a product that could help the communities and the businesses in it. Honestly, this wasn’t the difficult part because everything was around me and I was a part of it. I began to look for a problem and then a solution for it.

Hoping it not to be a one-time thing, I needed to come up with something that’s sustainable, easy to use and hassle free. The lifestyles have changed, things are faster and bigger today. And that’s only one side of the coin. A major part of our country is middle class, educated and still very authentic. Instead of moving away from it, why not stay authentic by connecting the dots through technology and build stronger communities?

That’s how the journey of A’lo began, going through various stages of development and with the help of all the amazing people around me.
